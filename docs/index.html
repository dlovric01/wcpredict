Predict the World Cup with friends

A free prediction game for the 2026 FIFA World Cup.

For every match, pick the score, who scores first, and a goalscorer. Lock in before kickoff and watch your points climb in real time as goals go in. Private groups, live leaderboards, knockout boosters.

What you can do

  • Predict every World Cup 2026 match — group stage and knockouts.
  • Earn up to 15 points per match (exact score + first team to score + goalscorer).
  • Apply round boosters in the knockouts: ×2 (R32) up to ×5 (Semi-Final). Final auto-×6.
  • Bonus tournament picks: WC Winner (+75) and Golden Boot (+50).
  • Create or join private groups with an 8-character invite code.

Sign-in

Sign in with Apple or Google. No email/password to manage, no ads, no tracking.

Get it

Download for Android (19 MB)

After tapping the file on your phone, Android will ask you to allow installs from this source — approve it, then install as normal. Works on any phone from 2017 onwards (arm64).
