Support

Need a hand? Read first — most issues are covered below.

Contact

Email support@wcpredict.app. We reply within a couple of business days.

Common questions

I can't sign in

  • Make sure you're online — Apple/Google ID verification needs a working network connection.
  • If “Sign in with Apple” doesn't appear, you may be on an iOS version older than 13. iOS 13 or later is required.
  • Try signing in with the other provider (Apple ↔ Google). Each creates a separate account.

My prediction wasn't scored

  • Scoring fires when a match reaches “Final” status. There may be a short delay (usually under a minute) after the final whistle.
  • If a goal is later overturned by VAR, scores re-compute automatically.
  • Predictions submitted after kickoff are locked out — they don't count.

I want to leave or delete a group

  • Open the group → top-right menu → Leave group.
  • Group owners can delete the entire group from the same menu.

Delete my account

Email us from the account address — we delete everything within 30 days. An in-app self-service option is on the roadmap.

Bug reports / feature requests

Email support@wcpredict.app with a short description and (if possible) a screenshot.
