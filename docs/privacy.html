Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

WC Predict (“the app”) is a free prediction game for the 2026 FIFA World Cup. This policy describes what we collect, why, and your rights.

Data we collect

When you sign in with Apple or Google we receive and store:

  • Email address — to identify your account.
  • Display name — visible to other members of groups you join.
  • User ID — a UUID generated by our backend (Supabase).

We also store the predictions you make and the groups you join so the app can score them and show the leaderboard.

What we do not collect

  • Photos, videos, contacts, calendars.
  • Location, health, fitness, financial data.
  • Browsing or search history.
  • Device or advertising identifiers (no IDFA usage).
  • Crash reports, analytics events, performance telemetry.

Why we collect it

Solely to operate the prediction game: identifying you, scoring your predictions, showing leaderboards in private groups, and replying to support requests you send us.

Sharing

  • Supabase (supabase.com) hosts the database and authentication on our behalf, as a data processor in the EU region.
  • api-sports.io provides FIFA fixture and match data. We do not send any of your personal data to them.
  • Apple and Google verify your identity when you tap “Sign in with Apple” / “Sign in with Google”. Their handling of that step is governed by their own privacy policies.

We do not sell or rent your data. We have no advertising partners. No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs are bundled with the app.

Tracking

We do not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use the IDFA. The App Tracking Transparency prompt is not shown because no tracking takes place.

Retention

We keep your account data until you delete your account. Predictions for finished matches remain in scoring tables until you delete the account, after which they are removed within 30 days.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, correction, or deletion at any time by emailing support@wcpredict.app. We respond within 30 days. Under GDPR you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

Children

WC Predict is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has signed in, email us and we will delete the account.

Changes to this policy

Material changes will be announced in-app and on this page. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
